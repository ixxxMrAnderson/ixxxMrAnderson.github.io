About Harvey

Harvey was born on May 4—a date regarded as Chinese version of the Enlightenment, so we wish him a free, and inquisitive spirit. He's already huge for a three-month-old Border Collie, yet he still gets bullied in the dog park. At home, he's learning to get along with Jesper (a meow), to coax extra treats from us, and to chew things without getting caught.
